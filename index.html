Gosple artist-Titilayomi
 
   
Titilayo Ibitayo Jenyo known as Titilayomi,is a Nigerian gosple musician and song writter,started singing in a cultural setting in the kings palace at Kabba Kogi state and moved to singing gosple songs after accepting Jesus as her lord and personal saviour in 1997 at redeem people mission church as a choirister from there she joined living faith church youth choir, Jos in plateau state when she gained admission into the university of Jos and later moved to the main chior where she is till serving God till date . Titilayomi is one of the main praise and worship leaders by privilege,she is the writter and singer of HIS LOVE,OLORUKO NLA,YABO NA,EBUBE DIKE,WO MO SEUN,etc

     
Titilayomi has a very uplifting voice that normaly moves God to do wonders during her praise and worship sessions.
